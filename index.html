The use of chemical substances such as azamethiphos, deltamethrin, teflubenzuron, emamectin and cypermethrin, to mitigate the development of parasites amongst farm-raised salmon populations can pose serious consequences for the marine environment. At high enough concentrations such chemicals can represent a potential hazard to farmed fish, local flora and fauna [1-3] as well as humans, e.g., water ingestion by swimmers [4].

The CLAWS Github repository aims at developing the necessary computational tools to allow practitioners to model chemicals, sea lice and waste from salmon farms. The code is open-source, under a GPL-2.0 license, and the platform is meant to be collaborative: if you would like to contribute, use it for educational purposes or need support in the form of consulting services, please get in touch.
